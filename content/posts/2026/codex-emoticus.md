# Introduction

I often find myself using a particular emoji to indicate a feeling, vibe, or "canned reaction" to something online. Oftentimes these can be open to interpretation[^1], so I've attempted to capture my own fun descriptors here. The list is by no means complete or accurate.

# The Codex

- 👀 Awkward. Basically [this meme](https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/awkward-look-monkey-puppet).
- 💪🏻 "Good work (especially athletically)!"
- 🎯 "Nailed it." "Spot on." "Exactly."
- 💯 "Strong agreement!"
- ‼️ "This is extra important" or "This is more important than it initially appears" ([associated sound](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=An7ZD-s6aHY))
- 🙈 Embarrassment.
